HELL HEAVEN

Designed by ASILA CUSTOMZ

Kenyan matatu culture (local name for Minibus) has gone to another level, with the introduction of a minibus nicknamed 'HELL HEAVEN'. It's fitted with 20 flat-screen televisions set inside with a 75" screen connected to a PS5..

HELL HEAVEN has now been the game changer making Kenya even much recognized worldwide as among the few countries that pimp public transport vehicles. Image Fifa being the most played game across Nairobi streets now passenger can even enjoy it either playing or watching it even their way to work if they aboard the bus.

The audiovisual entertainment was done by source entertainment and it comprises of a 75" screen at the front connected to a playstation ( ps5 ) and 20 flat screen TV and the best sound system imported from Japan.

  • 2 Rockford Fosgate T2500 Monoblock
  • 2 Rockford Fosgate T2 16 inch subs
Hell Heaven Representin Umoja Route 35-60... Designed by @diabloasila.... Airbrushed by @martin_ojiambo...

70% of Umoja Residents use the matatus as there daily means of transport to the city center from Umoja , and with the increase in the Matatu business, investors are looking for methods of attracting customers through different graffiti designers, resulting to attractive matatus such as hell heaven. Hell heaven aka " Kaka mbweha" belong to the gorilla family and is registered under salty Investment Sacco.
